# V22 Exit Criteria

文档状态：scoped accepted exit criteria。  
当前日期：2026-06-15。

## Passed

V22 may pass only if:

- V22.0-V22.6 all have explicit evidence；
- bad V21 pixel candidate is rejected；
- weak-motion fixture is rejected；
- technical-invalid fixture is rejected；
- repeated failure shows better-photo or alternate-route guidance；
- at least one approved candidate previews and applies target-only；
- rejected candidates cannot apply；
- rollback restores previous visible pack；
- final HTML embeds accepted and rejected visual evidence；
- security scan passes；
- claim scan passes。

## Blocked

V22 is blocked if:

- no approved candidate exists and the stage requires approved apply evidence；
- manual visual review cannot be performed；
- quality gates cannot distinguish rejected from approved candidates；
- evidence cannot safely show accepted/rejected examples。

## Failed

V22 fails if:

- rejected candidate can apply；
- QA failed pack appears in installable gallery；
- previous active pack is overwritten by bad asset；
- final report claims visual/product quality without visual acceptance；
- forbidden claims are used as ready claims。
